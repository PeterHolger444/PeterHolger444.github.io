Top 10 Pieces of Music That I Like

1. Notixx - Catch & Release

A very modern dubstep music. I like it because I can feel the adrenaline rush when listening to it.


2. Tartini Violin Sonata in G minor "Devil's Trill Sonata"

It's an emotional piece and the best-known composition of Giuseppe Tartini.


3. Barber of Seville - Pietro Spagnoli

I heard this song first from Tom & Jerry. A very fun piece to listen to.


4. Sergei Prokofiev - Battle On The Ice

An intense and powerful piece. It is a film score about Prince Alexander Nevsky defending Rus from the 13th-century incursion of the Livonian Order.


5. Sergei Prokofiev - Crusaders In Pskov

Another powerful piece from Sergei Prokofiev. There is a chorus in both “Battle on the Ice” and “Crusaders in Pskov” that goes "Peregrinus expectavi pedes meos in cymbalis". Some translate it as "As a foreigner, our feet are clad in cymbals" where cymbals are used as metaphor for the ice of the Lake Peipus. However, many have noted that it is a grammatically incorrect, nonsensical Latin phrase. Still, I think it fits well with the rest of the music.


6. The Logical Song - Supertramp

My favourite song with my favourite lyrics. The song touches on our modern life. Since childhood, we’ve been taught to concern ourselves only with questions that can be answered scientifically or empirically. And, thus we become not wanting to think of topics that cannot be answered using these methods: morality, religion, meaning and the self — since we think those are useless or waste of time. However, deep down we know that those topics are drilled into our existence and simply cannot turn away from them. "I know it sounds absurd but please tell me who I am...".


7. Violet Vape - Cheel

A great piece of music. It sounds very nostalgic for me.


8. Autumn Leaves - Housecat & Sisyphus 55, Vol. 2

It's an emotional piece. It's calm but upbeat at the same time.


9. Strangers At The Airport - Housecat & Sisyphus 55, Vol. 2

Another great song. I found this song and Autumn Leaves from one of the videos of the youtube channel Sisyphus 55. It's a great channel that covers philosophical and psychological topics.


10. Libet's delay - The Caretaker

It’s a very sad piece from the album An Empty Bliss Beyond This World. Libet's delay refers to the time between a person's skin being touched and the resulting conscious experience of being touched
