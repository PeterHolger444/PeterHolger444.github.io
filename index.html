CTF

CTF challenges are great ways to practice computer security practically. You can see my CTFtime profile here.

The followings are some CTFs I enjoy playing:

  • OverTheWire

    Wargames offered by OverTheWire community.

    Topics include: linux, web, cryptography and more. A great starting point for beginners.

  • Picoctf

    Online computer security competition for students and beginners to learn and practice. You can even solve challenges from past competitions in picoGYM.

    Covered a variety of topics: Binary Exploitation, Cryptography, Forensics, General Skills, Reverse Engineering and Web Exploitation.

  • CryptoHack

    Platform for cryptography related challenges.

  • Cryptopals

    Challenges to practice attacks on real-world cryptography.
